---
title: Rose AI Search Tips
description: Brief Rose clearly and refine in conversation to get sharper shortlists faster.
---

**Be specific, but conversational.** Rose responds well to natural language that describes the role clearly. Include seniority, location, industry, and any non-negotiables upfront.

> Good: *“I need a founding AE with 4+ years B2B SaaS experience, based in London or remote UK”*
>
> Less effective: *“sales person”*

**Use follow-up prompts to refine.** If Rose's first packages aren't quite right, keep the conversation going — tell her what you liked and what to change, and she'll iterate.

**Review multiple packages.** Rose often returns two or more packages with different interpretations of your brief. Each represents a distinct candidate profile worth considering.

**Check the reserve.** When a package's first batch doesn't produce enough interviews, ask Rose to show you more from the same package.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Fill out your [Knowledge Base](/getting-started/knowledge-base) once and Rose carries your company context into every search — so you don't have to repeat it.
</Callout>
