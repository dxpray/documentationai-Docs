## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">
Before you begin, make sure you have access to your email inbox and calendar credentials. The entire setup process takes less than five minutes.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Go to `clients.gethiros.com` and click **Sign up**. Enter your first name, last name, email address, and optionally your phone number. Accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, then click **Continue**.

## Verify Your Email

After registration you will receive a 6-digit verification code. Enter the code on the verification screen to activate your account. If you provided a phone number, complete the SMS verification step as well.

## Connect Your Calendar

Rose automatically books intro calls, so connecting a calendar is required.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Google Calendar" icon="calendar">
    Select Google Calendar and follow the OAuth prompts to grant Hiros access to your schedule.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Outlook Calendar" icon="calendar">
    Choose Outlook Calendar and authorize Hiros through your Microsoft account.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Zoom" icon="video">
    Connect Zoom to automatically generate meeting links for every booked call.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Set Up Your Knowledge Base

After calendar connection, upload your company information, job descriptions, and ideal candidate profiles. Rose uses this data to personalize outreach at scale.

````json
{
  "company": "Acme Corp",
  "roles": ["Senior Backend Engineer"],
  "tone": "professional yet friendly"
}
````

## Launch Your First Campaign

Once your knowledge base is ready, describe your target candidate to Rose and launch your first autonomous sourcing campaign.
