Rose generates a personalised three-email sequence based on your job details. You'll find it in the **Outreach** tab of each sequence, where every email is **personalised per candidate**.

## The three emails

| Email | When it sends |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Email 1 — Initial Outreach** | Sends first |
| **Email 2 — Follow-up** | If there's no reply |
| **Email 3 — Final Follow-up** | A final, brief touch |

Emails go out over several days during business hours, on a schedule you can see and adjust (days of the week, hours, and timezone) from the Outreach tab.

## Editing the copy

Each email has an editable **subject** and **body**. Personalisation tokens such as `{{firstName}}` are filled in automatically for every candidate. Use **Generate Email Sequence** to have Rose draft the set, or regenerate at any point.

<Callout kind="info">
  The opening and closing of each email are managed by Rose and optimised for deliverability, so they aren't editable. The main body is yours to shape.
</Callout>

## Staying out of spam

Rose runs a built-in **spam check** as you edit. If it finds risky words, it flags them as removable chips and offers **Fix all with Rose**. When you're clear, you'll see *“Spam check · you're in the clear.”*

When editing, avoid:

- Hype language (“revolutionary”, “game-changing”, “once-in-a-lifetime”)
- Urgency or pressure tactics (“last chance”, “urgent response needed”)

These patterns reduce deliverability and response rates.

When you launch, you'll get a final **Review your outreach** preview of all three emails before anything sends.
