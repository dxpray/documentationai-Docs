Open any sequence to manage it. The detail page shows the sequence title and status, an optional metrics bar, and a row of tabs.

## The tabs

| Tab | What's there |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Leads** | The full candidate pipeline, grouped by stage |
| **Ready to Book** | Candidates who've shared availability and are ready for a call |
| **Interviews booked** | Interviews already scheduled from this sequence |
| **Job Details** | The role information you entered, plus the **Reply automation** setting |
| **Outreach** | The emails Rose sends — view and edit before launching ([details](/sequences/outreach-emails)) |
| **ATS Integration** | Push candidates to your connected ATS (shown if ATS is available on your plan) |

## How the pipeline flows

In the **Leads** tab, candidates are grouped by where they are in the journey. Each stage has a plain-English meaning:

- **Sourced** — found by Rose; email lookup in progress.
- **Contacted** — first email sent, waiting on a reply.
- **Replied** — in conversation.
- **Interested** — showed interest; Rose is moving them forward.
- **Ready to book** — shared availability; pick a slot.
- **Interviews booked** — interview confirmed on your calendar.
- **Email not found** — no verified address; try LinkedIn instead.
- **Not interested** — passed on the role.
- **Rejected** — removed from this sequence.

You can select candidates to remove or export them, and add more candidates to a sequence at any time — before or after launching — from [Rose AI Search](/finding-candidates/rose-ai-search) or [Manual Filters](/finding-candidates/manual-filters).

## Launching

A draft sequence shows a **Launch sequence** button. Rose recommends having 10 or more candidates before you launch. You'll review the outreach emails and pick a [reply-automation mode](/how-rose-works/levels-of-control) before going live.

<Callout kind="info">
  Reply automation lives in the **Job Details** tab and can be changed any time, even after launch.
</Callout>
