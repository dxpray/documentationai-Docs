---
title: Sequence Analytics
description: Track performance across your pipeline — from first contact to interview booked — at both the sequence and account level.
---

Hiros gives you two levels of analytics: a live funnel inside each sequence, and rolled-up stats across all of them.

- **[The Sequence Funnel](/sequence-analytics/the-funnel)** — the metrics bar on a sequence's detail page.
- **[Account-Level Stats](/sequence-analytics/account-stats)** — the totals across every sequence you run.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Treat your **Not interested** responses as market intelligence — they often reveal how the market sees the role, the compensation, or the company.
</Callout>
