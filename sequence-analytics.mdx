Track performance across your pipeline — from first contact to interview booked.

Every sequence has a live analytics bar at the top of its detail page, showing a full funnel breakdown:

| Metric | What it means |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Sourced** | Total candidates added to the sequence |
| **Email Found** | Candidates for whom a valid email address was found |
| **Replied** | Candidates who replied to any email in the sequence |
| **Interested** | Candidates who expressed positive interest |
| **Ready to Book** | Candidates who have confirmed availability for a call |
| **Interviews Booked** | Confirmed intro calls |
| **Not Interested** | Candidates who declined |
| **Rejected** | Candidates removed from the sequence |

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use the **Not Interested** responses as market intelligence — they often reveal useful signals about how the market perceives the role, the compensation, or the company.
</Callout>

The **Sequences** overview page also shows aggregate stats across all your sequences: total active sequences, overall reply rate, and total interviews booked.
