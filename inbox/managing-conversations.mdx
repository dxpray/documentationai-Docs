title: Managing Conversations
description: Navigate, filter, and reply to candidates in the three-panel Inbox.
---

The Inbox has three panels: filters on the left, your conversation list in the middle, and the selected conversation thread on the right.

## Finding the right conversations

- **Needs you** shows only conversations that require your input — a reply Rose has drafted and is waiting to send, or an unusual question she's escalated. On “Rose replies automatically” this is often empty.
- **All** shows every conversation across all sequences, including those Rose has handled.
- **Filter by sequence** using the colour-coded list, and **search** by name, sequence, or message text.

Each conversation carries a status chip so you can see at a glance what's going on: **Draft ready** (Rose has a reply waiting for approval), **Needs you** (your input is needed), or **Rose handled** (already taken care of). A badge — **Auto**, **Semi-auto**, or **Manual** — shows the reply mode for that thread.

## Replying

Select a conversation and type in the reply box at the bottom of the thread. If you're on “Rose drafts, you approve,” Rose's draft appears ready for you to **Approve**, **Edit**, or **Reject**. You can also send your own message at any time.

<Callout kind="info">
  Once you reply yourself, Rose hands that conversation over to you and won't send on top of you.
</Callout>

## Candidate context and booking

Each thread shows the candidate's profile — photo, current role, location, contact email, summary, and work history — so you can assess fit before replying. When a candidate is ready, use **Book interview** right from the thread header. See [Booking Manually](/booking-interviews/booking-manually).
