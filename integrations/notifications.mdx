Connect **Slack** so Rose can keep you posted where your team already works.

| Integration | What it does |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Slack** | Rose notifies you in Slack when new candidates are ready for review. |

## Setting it up

Click **Connect** on the Slack card and authorise the connection. Once linked, choose the **Slack Channel** where Rose should post. Rose joins the channel so she can send messages — you'll see *“Rose has joined the channel”* when it's set.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Don't see the channel you want? Create it in Slack first, then refresh the channel list.
</Callout>
