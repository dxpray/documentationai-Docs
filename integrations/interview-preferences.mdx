Interview Preferences tell Rose how to schedule on your behalf. Open them with **Configure** in the Scheduling section of the [Integrations](/integrations) page.

## What you can set

| Setting | What it controls |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Scheduling Mode** | Whether Rose books automatically, suggests times for you to confirm, or just notifies you. See [How Rose Books Interviews](/booking-interviews/how-rose-books). |
| **Availability** | The days and hours you're open to interview. |
| **Interview Duration** | How long each interview should last. |
| **Buffer Between Meetings** | Free time Rose leaves between consecutive calls. |
| **Advance Booking Limit** | How far into the future Rose can schedule. |
| **Minimum Notice** | The least lead time before a call can be booked. |
| **Max Bookings / Day** | The most interviews Rose will book in a single day. |
| **Primary Calendar** | Which connected calendar Rose adds events to. |
| **Timezone** | The timezone all availability and booking times are shown in. |

<Callout kind="info">
  Only connected calendars appear in the **Primary Calendar** list — connect a calendar first under [Scheduling](/integrations/scheduling).
</Callout>
