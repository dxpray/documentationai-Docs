Hiros connects with a wide range of applicant tracking systems — **50+ platforms**, including Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, Workable, BambooHR, Bullhorn, SmartRecruiters, Teamtailor, Workday, iCIMS, JazzHR, Recruitee, Zoho Recruit, and many more.

## Connecting your ATS

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find your ATS" icon="search">
    Open the **Applicant Tracking** section of the Integrations page and locate your platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect" icon="plug">
    Click **Connect** and follow the steps in the pop-up to authorise the link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your account" icon="user-check">
    Choose the correct ATS account for your organisation — especially in teams where colleagues have their own logins.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  ATS integration availability can depend on your plan. If a card shows **Upgrade to unlock**, see [Billing & Subscription](/billing-and-subscription/plans).
</Callout>

## Pushing candidates

Once connected, you can push candidates to your ATS in two places: from the **ATS Integration** tab inside a [sequence](/sequences/managing-a-sequence), or with **Add to ATS** from a candidate's conversation in the [Inbox](/inbox).
