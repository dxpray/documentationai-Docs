---
title: Scheduling Integrations
description: Connect Google Calendar, Outlook, or Zoom so Rose can book intro calls and attach meeting links.
---

The **Scheduling** section is where you connect the calendar and video tools Rose uses to book interviews.

| Integration | What it does |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Google Calendar** | Rose books intro calls directly in your Google Calendar. |
| **Outlook Calendar** | Rose schedules meetings and sends invites through Outlook. |
| **Zoom** | Rose creates Zoom meetings and attaches the links to your bookings. |

Click **Connect** next to an integration and follow the authorisation steps. A connected calendar can be disconnected at any time.

<Callout kind="warning">
  At least one calendar must be connected for Rose to book interviews on your behalf.
</Callout>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Using a personal Outlook account (such as @outlook.com or @hotmail.com)? A Teams link isn't always added to invites automatically — connecting Zoom as well ensures a call link is always attached.
</Callout>

To set the rules Rose follows when booking, use **Configure** in this section to open your [Interview Preferences](/integrations/interview-preferences).
