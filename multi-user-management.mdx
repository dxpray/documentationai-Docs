Add your colleagues to Hiros so your whole team can collaborate on hiring.

## Accessing Organisation Settings

Click **Settings** in the left sidebar (the gear icon), then navigate to **Organisation → Members**.

## Inviting Team Members

Click **Invite** in the top right of the Members panel. Enter your colleague's email address and they will receive an invitation to join your organisation.

Pending invitations are visible under the **Invitations** tab, where you can track or cancel them.

## Managing Roles

Each member is assigned a role, visible in the **Role** column of the Members table. Currently available roles include:

- **Admin** — full access to all features, settings, billing, and team management

To change a member's role, click the dropdown next to their name in the Role column.

## Removing Members

Click the **...** (actions) button next to a member's row to access options including removing them from the organisation.

## Organisation Settings

Under **Organisation → General**, you can:

- **Update your organisation profile** — name and logo
- **Leave the organisation** — removes your own access
- **Delete the organisation** — permanently removes the organisation and all associated data (irreversible)

<Callout kind="warning">
  Deleting the organisation permanently removes it and all associated data. This action is irreversible.
</Callout>
