---
title: Multi-User Management
description: Add your colleagues to Hiros so your whole team can collaborate on hiring.
---

Hiros is built for teams. Everyone in your organisation shares the same sequences, candidates, integrations, and billing.

- **[Inviting Your Team](/multi-user-management/inviting-your-team)** — bring colleagues into your organisation.
- **[Roles & Permissions](/multi-user-management/roles-and-permissions)** — control what members can do.
- **[Organisation Settings](/multi-user-management/organisation-settings)** — manage your organisation profile and membership.

<Callout kind="info">
  Team and organisation management is reached from the **organisation switcher** at the bottom of the left sidebar. Click it to manage your organisation or switch between organisations you belong to.
</Callout>
