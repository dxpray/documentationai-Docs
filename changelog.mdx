## Overview

This is the highlight reel of big changes to Hiros — the features that changed how you source, engage, and book candidates. We ship smaller improvements and fixes continuously between these milestones.

<Update label="June 2026" description="Sequence workflow & billing" tags={["feature", "improvement"]}>

## Sequences, reimagined around Rose

- **Start a sequence straight from Rose.** The home screen now lets you pick a new or existing sequence before you start a search, so candidates flow into the right place from the first message.
- **Add candidates to ongoing sequences.** Top up a live sequence with new candidates at any time — no need to start over.
- **Editable company details** on a sequence's job details.

## Billing

- **Seat-based billing and local currency** — plans now reflect your team size and show pricing in your currency.

</Update>

<Update label="May 2026" description="Rose AI Search" tags={["feature"]}>

## Meet Rose, your conversational recruiter

- **Rose AI Search.** Describe who you're hiring in plain language and Rose researches, sources, and returns curated **Candidate Packages** — a fundamentally faster way to find people than manual filters.
- **New credit system.** A clearer, usage-based model so you can see exactly what each action costs.
- **French-language outreach.** Rose can now run outreach in French as well as English.
- **CSV contact export.** Export candidate contacts directly for use elsewhere.

</Update>

<Update label="April 2026" description="Control & setup" tags={["feature", "improvement"]}>

## More control over how Rose works

- **Reply automation modes.** Choose how much Rose runs on her own — reply automatically, draft for your approval, or stay hands-off while you reply yourself.
- **Import a job description.** Upload a JD to auto-fill a new sequence in seconds.
- **Create and manage sequences from search.** Spin up, edit, and delete sequences as a natural part of sourcing.

</Update>

<Update label="March 2026" description="Notifications & sourcing" tags={["feature", "improvement"]}>

## Stay in the loop, source more precisely

- **Slack notifications.** Rose pings you in Slack when candidates are ready for review.
- **Cancel sequences** whenever you need to stop outreach.
- **Automatic decline emails.** Candidates you pass on receive a courteous note, automatically.
- **Richer natural-language search** — target multiple companies and titles in a single brief.

</Update>

<Update label="October 2025" description="Booking & conversations" tags={["feature"]}>

## From outreach to booked calls

- **Interview booking system.** Rose turns interested candidates into scheduled intro calls on your calendar.
- **Rose handles replies.** Rose reads candidate responses, understands intent, and replies on your behalf.
- **AI-personalised outreach emails.** Every message is tailored to the candidate and the role.

</Update>

<Update label="September 2025" description="Automated outreach" tags={["feature"]}>

## Multi-touch email outreach

- **Automated outreach sequences** — Rose sends a multi-step email campaign to your selected candidates.

</Update>

<Update label="June–August 2025" description="Hiros launches" tags={["feature"]}>

## The first release

- **AI candidate sourcing and screening** — search a database of hundreds of millions of professionals and review matched candidates.
- **Client onboarding** — set up your account, organisation, and calendar.
- **Email verification at sign-up** for secure access.

</Update>
