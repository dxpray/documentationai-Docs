Connect your calendar, video conferencing, ATS, and notification tools to get the most out of Hiros.

## Accessing Integrations

Click **Integrations** in the left sidebar (the puzzle piece icon). The page shows all connected and available integrations — currently 57 available across four categories.

## Scheduling

| Integration | What it does |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Google Calendar** | Rose books intro calls directly into your Google Calendar |
| **Outlook Calendar** | Rose schedules meetings and sends invites via Outlook |
| **Zoom** | Rose creates Zoom meeting links and attaches them to bookings |

To connect, click **Connect** next to the relevant integration and follow the authorisation steps. Google Calendar will show **Disconnect** once connected.

<Callout kind="info">
  At least one calendar integration is required for Rose to book interviews on your behalf.
</Callout>

## Notifications

| Integration | What it does |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Slack** | Rose will notify you in Slack when new candidates are ready for review |

## Applicant Tracking (ATS)

Hiros integrates with 57+ ATS platforms, including Ashby, BambooHR, Breezy HR, Bullhorn, Clockwork, Comeet, Cornerstone TalentLink, Crelate, Dayforce, d.Vinci, Easycruit, EngageATS, and many more.

**To connect your ATS:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find your ATS" icon="search">
    Locate your ATS in the Applicant Tracking section of the Integrations page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check API access" icon="key">
    Ensure you have an ATS plan that includes API access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect" icon="plug">
    Click **Connect** and follow the instructions in the pop-up.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once connected, you can push candidates to your ATS directly from within a sequence (via the **ATS Integration** tab) or from a candidate's conversation panel.

<Callout kind="warning">
  Make sure you select the correct ATS account for your organisation — especially in team environments where colleagues may have their own ATS logins.
</Callout>
