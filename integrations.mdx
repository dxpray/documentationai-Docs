title: Integrations
description: Connect your calendar, video, notifications, and ATS so Rose can book calls and manage meetings on your behalf.
---

Click **Integrations** in the left sidebar to open **Your Integrations**. The header shows how many tools are connected and how many are available, and integrations are grouped into clear sections.

## In this section

- **[Scheduling](/integrations/scheduling)** — Google Calendar, Outlook, and Zoom.
- **[Interview Preferences](/integrations/interview-preferences)** — the rules Rose follows when booking.
- **[Notifications](/integrations/notifications)** — Slack alerts when candidates are ready.
- **[Applicant Tracking (ATS)](/integrations/applicant-tracking)** — push candidates to your ATS.

<Callout kind="info">
  To connect a tool, click **Connect** on its card and follow the prompts. Once connected, the card offers **Disconnect**. You can manage all of this any time without affecting your live sequences.
</Callout>
