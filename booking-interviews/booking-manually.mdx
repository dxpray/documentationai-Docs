title: Booking Manually
description: Pick a time yourself with the Book Intro Call flow, from a conversation or the sequence pipeline.
---

To book a call yourself, open the candidate's conversation in the [Inbox](/inbox) (or find them in a sequence's pipeline) and click **Book interview** / **Book Intro Call**.

## The booking flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a date and time" icon="calendar">
    Choose a date, then a time slot. Available times are pulled from your connected calendar, and you can set the meeting duration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the details" icon="check-circle">
    Review the candidate, date, time, duration, timezone, and which calendar the event will be added to. Optionally add **Notes for the candidate** — prep tips or topics for the call.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done" icon="party-popper">
    Click **Confirm booking**. The invite is added to your calendar (with a video link if you've connected Zoom), and the candidate receives a confirmation email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After confirming, you'll see a **Booking Confirmed** screen noting that a confirmation email is on its way, along with links to the calendar event.

<Callout kind="info">
  Need to change a booking? You can **Reschedule** or **Cancel** an interview from the [Bookings page](/booking-interviews/the-bookings-page).
</Callout>
