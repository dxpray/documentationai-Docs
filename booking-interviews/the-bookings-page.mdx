Click **Bookings** in the left sidebar to open **Your Interviews** — every scheduled interview in one place.

## Summary and filters

The top of the page shows quick counts: **Today**, **Tomorrow**, **This week**, **Confirmed** (with your confirmation rate), and **Past**. Use the **Upcoming / Past / All** tabs to switch views; upcoming interviews are grouped into Today, Tomorrow, This week, Next week, and Later.

## Each interview

Every row shows the time, the candidate's name and headline, the duration, and the meeting type (such as a Zoom link), along with a status. Open a row's menu for quick actions: **Join call**, **Copy link**, **Edit**, **Reschedule**, and **Cancel call**.

A side panel highlights what's **Up next** and what's on for **Today**, so your next call is always one glance away.

## Calendar Health

The **Calendar health** panel is a live status check of your connected tools — Google Calendar, Zoom, and Outlook — showing which are synced and which need attention.

<Callout kind="warning">
  If Calendar Health shows a disconnected integration, reconnect it on the [Integrations](/integrations/scheduling) page. Rose can't book interviews without an active calendar connection.
</Callout>
