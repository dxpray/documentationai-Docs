The Inbox is where you manage all candidate conversations. Rose handles most replies automatically — what lands here is only what needs your attention.

## Overview

Click the **Inbox** icon (speech bubble) in the left sidebar to open the Conversations view.

The Inbox has three panels:

- **Left sidebar** — filter by inbox type (Needs you / All) and by individual sequence
- **Middle panel** — list of conversations, with candidate name, sequence, message preview, and a "Rose handled" tag where applicable
- **Right panel** — the full conversation thread for the selected candidate

## Needs You vs. All

**Needs you** shows only conversations that require your input — for example, a candidate reply that Rose has drafted a response to but is waiting for your approval, or an unusual query that Rose has escalated. If you're on the "Rose replies automatically" mode, this section will often be empty.

**All** shows every conversation across all sequences, including those Rose has already handled.

The right panel shows: "Rose surfaces what needs you on the left. Anything she can handle herself never makes it here."

## Filtering by Sequence

In the left sidebar, your active sequences are listed with colour-coded dots and reply counts. Click any sequence to filter the conversation list to candidates from that sequence only.

## Replying to Candidates

Select a conversation and type your reply in the text box at the bottom of the right panel. Click **Send** to send it immediately.

If you are on "Rose drafts, you approve" mode, Rose's drafted reply will appear pre-filled in the text box — you can edit it or send it as-is.

## Viewing Candidate Profiles

Each conversation panel shows the candidate's full profile on the left: photo, current role, location, contact email, professional summary, and work history. You can use this to assess their suitability before booking a call or replying.
