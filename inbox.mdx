The Inbox is where you manage all candidate conversations. Rose handles most replies automatically — what lands here is only what needs your attention.

## How Rose handles replies

Before a conversation ever reaches your Inbox, Rose has already read it and decided what to do. For every candidate reply she:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Checks it's genuine" icon="shield-check">
    Rose filters out auto-responders, spam, and attempts to manipulate her through email content — so noise never reaches you and never derails a campaign.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reads the intent" icon="message-circle">
    Rose works out what the candidate means — interested, asking questions, not interested, or asking to opt out — and moves them to the right pipeline stage automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pulls out availability" icon="calendar-clock">
    If the candidate suggests times, Rose extracts the dates, times, and time zone (inferring the zone from their location if it isn't stated) and kicks off booking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Responds in your voice" icon="reply">
    Rose drafts a reply — answering questions and, when appropriate, inviting the candidate to book — then sends it, queues it for your approval, or steps aside, depending on your settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  Opt-outs are honoured automatically. If a candidate asks to be removed, Rose stops contacting them and suppresses future outreach — you don't need to do a thing.
</Callout>

This is why the Inbox stays quiet: **anything Rose can handle, she handles**, and only the conversations that genuinely need a human land in front of you.

## Overview

Click the **Inbox** icon (speech bubble) in the left sidebar to open the Conversations view.

The Inbox has three panels:

- **Left sidebar** — filter by inbox type (Needs you / All) and by individual sequence
- **Middle panel** — list of conversations, with candidate name, sequence, message preview, and a "Rose handled" tag where applicable
- **Right panel** — the full conversation thread for the selected candidate

## Needs You vs. All

**Needs you** shows only conversations that require your input — for example, a candidate reply that Rose has drafted a response to but is waiting for your approval, or an unusual query that Rose has escalated. If you're on the "Rose replies automatically" mode, this section will often be empty.

**All** shows every conversation across all sequences, including those Rose has already handled.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Rose surfaces what needs you on the left. Anything she can handle herself never makes it here.
</Callout>

## Filtering by Sequence

In the left sidebar, your active sequences are listed with colour-coded dots and reply counts. Click any sequence to filter the conversation list to candidates from that sequence only.

## Replying to Candidates

Select a conversation and type your reply in the text box at the bottom of the right panel. Click **Send** to send it immediately.

If you are on "Rose drafts, you approve" mode, Rose's drafted reply will appear pre-filled in the text box — you can edit it or send it as-is.

<Callout kind="info">
  Once you step in and reply to a candidate yourself, Rose hands that particular conversation to you and won't auto-send on top of you — so your personal touch always takes precedence.
</Callout>

## Viewing Candidate Profiles

Each conversation panel shows the candidate's full profile on the left: photo, current role, location, contact email, professional summary, and work history. You can use this to assess their suitability before booking a call or replying.
