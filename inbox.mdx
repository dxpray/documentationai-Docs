The Inbox is where you manage all candidate conversations. Rose handles most replies automatically — what lands here is only what needs your attention. Open it with the **Inbox** icon in the left sidebar.

## In this section

- **[How Rose Handles Replies](/inbox/how-rose-handles-replies)** — what Rose does with every message before it reaches you.
- **[Managing Conversations](/inbox/managing-conversations)** — navigating, filtering, and replying in the Inbox.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Rose surfaces what needs you on the left. Anything she can handle herself never makes it here.
</Callout>
