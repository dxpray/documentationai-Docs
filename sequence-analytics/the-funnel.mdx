---

Every launched sequence has a metrics bar on its detail page. Use the **Show metrics** / **Hide metrics** toggle to expand it.

## What each metric means

| Metric | What it counts |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Email not found** | Candidates sourced but without a verified email address |
| **Contacted** | Candidates who have received outreach and are awaiting a reply |
| **Replied** | Candidates who have replied |
| **Interested** | Candidates who expressed positive interest |
| **Booked** | Candidates with a confirmed interview |
| **Not interested** | Candidates who declined |

For the complete, candidate-by-candidate view — including the **Ready to book** and **Rejected** stages — open the **Leads** tab, where every candidate is grouped by stage. See [Managing a Sequence](/sequences/managing-a-sequence).

<Callout kind="info">
  These numbers update live as Rose works, so the bar always reflects the current state of your pipeline.
</Callout>
