---
title: Account-Level Stats
description: See your hiring performance rolled up across every sequence.
---

The **Sequences** page shows headline stats across all of your active sequences, so you can gauge overall performance at a glance.

| Stat | What it shows |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Active** | How many sequences are currently in progress |
| **Reply rate** | The share of contacted candidates who have replied, across your sequences |
| **Interviews booked** | Total interviews booked across your sequences |

For a view centred on your calendar instead, the [Bookings page](/booking-interviews/the-bookings-page) breaks down interviews by Today, Tomorrow, This week, Confirmed, and Past.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Reply rate is the fastest signal of whether your targeting and messaging are landing. If it's low, revisit your [outreach copy](/sequences/outreach-emails) or tighten who you're sourcing.
</Callout>
