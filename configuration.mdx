## Overview

Configure Hiros to match your recruiting workflow. Update your knowledge base so Rose has accurate company context, manage calendar connections for seamless scheduling, and set team permissions to control access.

<Callout kind="info">
  Keep your knowledge base and integrations up to date. Outdated information reduces Rose's effectiveness when engaging candidates.
</Callout>

## Managing Your Knowledge Base

Your knowledge base helps Rose understand your company, roles, and hiring criteria. Update it regularly to reflect new job descriptions or company changes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Knowledge Base" icon="book-open">
    Navigate to **Settings** then select **Knowledge Base** from the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add or Edit Content" icon="edit">
    Enter details about your company mission, open roles, ideal candidate profiles, and any specific messaging guidelines.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes" icon="save">
    Click **Update Knowledge Base**. Rose will begin using the new information within minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Calendar and Video Conferencing Integrations

Connect your calendar so Rose can book meetings directly. You can also link video tools to automatically generate meeting links.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Google Calendar" icon="calendar">
    Go to **Integrations** and click **Connect** next to Google Calendar. Authorize access when prompted. Rose will create events in your primary calendar.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Outlook Calendar" icon="mail">
    Select Outlook from the integrations list and follow the Microsoft login flow. Once connected, Rose schedules meetings using your Outlook account.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Zoom" icon="video">
    Connect Zoom to generate meeting links automatically. Paste your Zoom credentials or authorize via OAuth when prompted.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Organization Settings and Team Permissions

Control who can access your Hiros workspace and what actions they can perform.

<ParamField body="team_member_email" param-type="string" required="true">
  Email address of the team member you want to invite.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="role" param-type="string" required="true">
  Assign either `admin` or `recruiter` permissions.
</ParamField>

Use the organization settings page to manage billing details, workspace name, and default notification preferences.
