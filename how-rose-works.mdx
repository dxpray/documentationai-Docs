Rose is the AI recruiter at the heart of Hiros. She sources real candidates, runs your outreach, holds the conversations, and books the calls — so you can focus on interviewing.

Most "AI recruiting" tools are search boxes with a chatbot bolted on. Rose is different. She works the way a world-class talent partner does: she takes a brief, thinks through who would actually be a fit, goes and finds them, reaches out in your voice, and manages every reply until a warm candidate is sitting on your calendar. You stay in control at every step — Rose just does the heavy lifting.

<Callout kind="info">
  You describe who you're looking for. Rose finds them, engages them, and fills your pipeline with warm, interested candidates — without you needing to lift a finger.
</Callout>

## How Rose thinks

When Rose evaluates whether a candidate fits, she weighs evidence in a deliberate order — much like an experienced recruiter would:

| Priority | What Rose looks for |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Task similarity** | Has this person actually done the work your role requires — not just held a similar title? |
| **Evidence of output** | Is there concrete proof of results, shipped work, or measurable impact? |
| **Problem exposure** | Have they worked on the kinds of problems, scale, or stage you're hiring for? |
| **Company context** | Does their background line up with your company's industry, stage, and size? |
| **Complexity** | Have they operated at the level of complexity the role demands? |

This is why Rose's results tend to feel hand-picked rather than scraped. She's reasoning about fit, not just filtering rows.

## The end-to-end workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tell Rose who you're hiring" icon="message-square">
    Describe the role in natural language in the Rose AI search, or build a search manually using filters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and select candidates" icon="users">
    Browse Rose's candidate packages or your filter results, and select who to engage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up a sequence" icon="list">
    Add job details and customise the outreach emails Rose will send.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch" icon="send">
    Rose reaches out to candidates and manages the conversation from there.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Book interviews" icon="calendar">
    When a candidate is interested, Rose books the call — or hands it to you, ready to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Built to be trusted

Rose treats messages from candidates as untrusted input and is hardened against attempts to manipulate her through email content, so an unusual reply can never hijack your campaign. And because Rose is AI and can occasionally make mistakes, Hiros keeps you in the loop where it matters: you review candidates before outreach, and you choose how much of the conversation Rose runs on her own.

<Callout kind="warning">
  Rose is powerful, but she's a co-pilot, not a black box. Always review profiles before adding them to a sequence, and pick the reply-automation mode that matches how much oversight you want.
</Callout>
