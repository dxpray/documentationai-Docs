## Overview

Rose is Hiros's AI agent that autonomously handles the full recruiting workflow. You define your ideal candidate profile once, and Rose sources, engages, and qualifies talent until they are ready for your team.

<Callout kind="info">
  Rose works 24/7, allowing you to focus on interviews instead of sourcing.
</Callout>

## Defining Your Ideal Candidate Criteria

Start by telling Rose exactly who you need. Navigate to the Roles section and create a new position.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create role" icon="plus">
    Enter the job title, department, and location preferences.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add requirements" icon="list">
    Specify must-have skills, experience level, and keywords. Use natural language descriptions for best results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set volume" icon="target">
    Choose how many candidates you want Rose to engage per week.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Automated Sourcing and Engagement

Rose continuously searches across multiple platforms using your criteria. She personalizes every message and follows up intelligently.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="LinkedIn" icon="linkedin">
    Rose identifies profiles matching your requirements and sends connection requests with personalized notes.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Email" icon="mail">
    When email addresses are available, Rose sends targeted outreach sequences with relevant context about your role.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Conversation Management Until Handoff

Rose handles all replies, answers questions about the role, and qualifies candidates based on your criteria. Conversations continue automatically until a candidate confirms interest in speaking with your team.

<Expandable title="What happens during handoff?" default-open="false">
  Once a candidate is ready, Rose proposes times from your connected calendar and creates the meeting invite with all context attached.
</Expandable>

## Pipeline Tracking and Collaboration

Monitor progress in real time through the Pipeline view. Share candidate profiles with teammates and add internal notes without disrupting Rose's workflow.
