Rose sources candidates, manages outreach, and handles conversations — so you can focus on the interviews.

## Rose as a Sourcing Agent

Rose is not a traditional search tool. She works like a world-class talent researcher: you describe who you're looking for in plain language, and she goes out and finds real matching profiles from a database of 800M+ indexed professionals.

Rather than returning a flat list of results, Rose organises her findings into **Candidate Packages** — curated groups of candidates, each with a descriptive label, so you can quickly understand who she's found and why.

## Rose as an Outreach Agent

Once you've selected candidates and launched a sequence, Rose takes over the conversation entirely. She sends a personalised, multi-touch email campaign on your behalf and responds to candidate replies — fielding questions, collecting availability, and notifying you when someone is ready to be booked.

Depending on your preferred level of control, you can choose how Rose handles replies:

- **Rose replies automatically** — end-to-end handling; Rose escalates only the tricky conversations

- **Rose drafts, you approve** — Rose drafts every reply, but it goes out only after you approve it

- **You reply yourself** — Rose stays quiet; you manage the conversation manually

## The End-to-End Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tell Rose who you're hiring" icon="message-square" title-type="p">
    Describe the role in natural language via the Rose AI search, or build a search manually using filters.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and select candidates" icon="users" title-type="p">
    Browse Rose's candidate packages or your filter results, and select who to engage.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up a sequence" icon="list" title-type="p">
    Add job details and customise the outreach emails Rose will send.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Launch" icon="send" title-type="p">
    Rose reaches out to candidates and manages the conversation from there.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Book interviews" icon="calendar" title-type="p">
    When a candidate is interested, you'll be notified to book an intro call.
  </Step>
</Steps>
