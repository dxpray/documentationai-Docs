Getting set up takes just a few minutes. There are three things to do before Rose can start sourcing and booking for you — create your account, connect your calendar, and tell Rose about your company.

<Callout kind="info">
  Hiros helps you engage top candidates for your open roles at scale. At the centre of the platform is **Rose** — an AI recruiter who sources real candidates, reaches out on your behalf, and manages conversations right up until a candidate is ready to speak to your team.
</Callout>

## The setup steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account" icon="user-plus">
    Sign up, verify your email, and create your organisation. See [Create an Account](/getting-started/create-account).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your calendar" icon="calendar">
    Link Google, Outlook, or Zoom so Rose can book intro calls. See [Connect Your Calendar](/getting-started/connect-calendar).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your Knowledge Base" icon="book-open">
    Introduce your company to Rose so she can tailor every search and message. See [Set Up Your Knowledge Base](/getting-started/knowledge-base).
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once you're set up, head to [Finding Candidates](/finding-candidates) to run your first search, or read [How Rose Works](/how-rose-works) to understand what Rose can do.

## Finding your way around

Everything in Hiros is reachable from the left sidebar:

| Item | What it's for |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Rose** | The home screen — start a search and chat with Rose |
| **Sequences** | Your outreach campaigns, one per role |
| **Inbox** | Candidate conversations that need your attention |
| **Bookings** | Your scheduled interviews |
| **Integrations** | Connect your calendar, Slack, and ATS |
| **Settings** | Billing, integrations, and your Knowledge Base |

Your credit balance sits at the bottom of the sidebar, with a **Top up** link and the date your credits reset.
