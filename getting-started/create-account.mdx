title: Create an Account

---

Creating your account takes seconds. You'll sign up, verify your email, and create the organisation that holds your team, sequences, and billing.

## Sign up

Go to [clients.gethiros.com](https://clients.gethiros.com) and enter your email to get started. You can sign up with your work email, and you'll be guided straight into the rest of setup.

If you already have an account, enter the same email to sign back in.

## Verify your email

To keep your account secure, Hiros sends a verification code to your email address. Enter the code on screen to confirm it's you and continue.

## Create your organisation

Next, you'll create your **organisation** — your company's workspace in Hiros. This is where your sequences, candidates, integrations, and billing all live, and it's what you'll invite teammates into later.

Give your organisation a name to create it. If you've been invited to an existing organisation, you can select it here instead.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Work with multiple clients as a recruiter or agency? You can switch between organisations any time using the organisation switcher at the bottom of the left sidebar. See [Multi-User Management](/multi-user-management).
</Callout>

With your organisation created, you'll move on to [connecting your calendar](/getting-started/connect-calendar).
