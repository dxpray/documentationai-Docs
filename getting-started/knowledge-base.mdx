title: Set Up Your Knowledge Base
description: Introduce your company to Rose so she can tailor every search and outreach message to who you are.
---

The Knowledge Base is what Rose knows about your company. She uses it to seed every chat — so her searches and outreach reflect your industry, stage, and story without you having to explain it each time.

## Introducing your company

Rose can get up to speed in about a minute. The quickest way is to give her your **company website** — she'll pull together what's publicly known, then let you review and edit everything before saving. You can also fill in the details by hand.

<Callout kind="tip">
  If you're a recruiter or agency working across multiple clients, you can skip this — Rose picks up context per sequence as you go.
</Callout>

## What you can tell Rose

The Knowledge Base captures:

- **Company name**
- **Website**
- **Industry**
- **Headcount** — a size band from 1 up to 10,000+
- **Headquarters**
- **Year founded**
- **Stage** — from Pre-seed and Seed through the Series rounds to Public, Bootstrapped, Non-profit, and more
- **Anything else Rose should know** — a free-text note for your hiring philosophy, ideal candidates, what makes you different, or companies you'd rather not approach

<Callout kind="info">
  Stage is the one required field — Rose uses it to bias every search toward the right kind of candidate. The free-text note is where the magic happens: the more context you give, the sharper Rose's sourcing and the more on-brand her outreach.
</Callout>

## Updating it later

Your Knowledge Base is never locked in. Update it any time under **Settings → Knowledge base**, and click **Save changes**. Rose picks up the changes on your next search.
