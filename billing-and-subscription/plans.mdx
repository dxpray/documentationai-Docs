Your plan is shown on the **Plans & credits** page, with its price, billing term, and renewal date. Use **Compare plans** to see your options side by side and switch.

## What your plan determines

- Your monthly credit allowance.
- The features available to you, such as ATS integrations.
- Other capacity limits suited to your hiring volume.

## Changing your plan

You can switch between **monthly** and **yearly** billing — yearly saves a percentage versus monthly. Changes and cancellations can be made at any time, and **Manage billing** opens your billing details and invoices.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Need something bespoke? Larger or custom plans are available — use the **Talk to sales** option on the compare-plans screen, or contact the Hiros team directly.
</Callout>
