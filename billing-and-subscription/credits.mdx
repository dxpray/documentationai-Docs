---
title: Credits
description: How Hiros measures usage with credits, and how to keep topped up.
---

Hiros uses a credit system to track usage. Your current balance is always visible at the bottom of the left sidebar, along with the date your credits reset — for example, *“3,950 / 4,000 left · Resets Jun 9.”* The colour shifts as you use more, so you can tell at a glance how much is left.

## How credits are used

Credits are consumed as Rose works on your behalf. The **Plans & credits** page breaks down the cost of each action — such as sourcing a candidate, exporting a contact, and when a candidate replies — alongside a clear view of how much of your allowance you've used this period.

## Topping up

If you're running low, click **Top up** next to the credit counter in the sidebar (or on the billing page) to add more credits.

<Callout kind="warning">
  Cancelling a sequence after it has launched doesn't refund credits already used on contacted candidates.
</Callout>
