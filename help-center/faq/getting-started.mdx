Getting up and running takes just a few minutes.

## Set up your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up" icon="user-plus">
    Go to [clients.gethiros.com](https://clients.gethiros.com) and sign up with your work email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email" icon="mail-check">
    Enter the verification code sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your organisation" icon="building">
    Name your organisation — your company's workspace for sequences, candidates, and billing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your calendar" icon="calendar">
    Link Google Calendar, Outlook, or Zoom so Rose can book intro calls.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your Knowledge Base" icon="book-open">
    Introduce your company to Rose so she can tailor every search and message.
  </Step>
</Steps>

For the full walkthrough, see [Getting Started](/getting-started).

## Run your first search

Open **Rose** from the left sidebar and describe who you're hiring in plain language — for example, *“a senior backend engineer in Berlin with fintech experience.”* Rose returns curated Candidate Packages you can review and add to a sequence. See [Rose AI Search](/finding-candidates/rose-ai-search).
