## Quick Start

To use Hiros for AI-native recruiting:

1. Go to `https://app.hiros.ai`.
2. Click "Sign Up" and complete your profile.
3. Connect your LinkedIn or upload a resume database.
4. Set your sourcing preferences (roles, skills).

## Access the Dashboard

Sign in to monitor matches, candidates, and activity.
