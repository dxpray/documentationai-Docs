---
title: Can't log in to Hiros
description: Resolve sign-in problems with Hiros.
---

Hiros signs you in with your email and a verification code. If you're having trouble getting in, work through these steps.

## Common fixes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check you're using the right email" icon="mail">
    Sign in at [clients.gethiros.com](https://clients.gethiros.com) with the email you signed up with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Didn't get your code?" icon="refresh-cw">
    Check your spam folder, wait a moment, and request a new code. Make sure the email address is typed correctly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wrong organisation?" icon="building">
    If you're in but don't see your team's sequences, you may be in the wrong workspace. Use the organisation switcher at the bottom of the left sidebar to change organisations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Still stuck?" icon="life-buoy">
    Email [support@gethiros.com](mailto:support@gethiros.com) from your registered address and we'll help you get back in.
  </Step>
</Steps>
