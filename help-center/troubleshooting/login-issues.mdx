---
title: Can't Log In to Hiros
description: Resolve login problems with Hiros.
---

## Symptoms

- "Incorrect email or password" error
- Login page reloads with no message

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check credentials">
    Make sure your email and password are correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset password">
    Click "Forgot password?" and follow the instructions sent to your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If you still can't access, email `support@hiros.ai` from your registered address.
  </Step>
</Steps>
