---
title: Scheduling an Interview with Hiros
description: Guide to automating your interview scheduling in Hiros.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a candidate">
    Go to the Candidates tab and click on the profile you want to interview.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose 'Schedule Interview'">
    Click the 'Schedule Interview' button on the candidate's page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick time and method">
    Select a time slot and video platform (Zoom, Meet, etc.)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send invite">
    Click 'Send'. Hiros will notify the candidate and add to your calendar automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
