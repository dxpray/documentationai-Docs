---
title: Scheduling an interview
description: How interviews get booked in Hiros — automatically by Rose, or manually by you.
---

When a candidate is interested and shares their availability, Rose can book the intro call for you — or hand it over ready for you to confirm. How much Rose does depends on your [Interview Preferences](/integrations/interview-preferences).

## Book a call yourself

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the conversation" icon="inbox">
    In the [Inbox](/inbox), select the candidate who's ready to meet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Book interview" icon="calendar-plus">
    Use the **Book interview** button in the conversation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a date and time" icon="clock">
    Choose from slots pulled from your connected calendar, and set the duration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm" icon="check-circle">
    Review the details, optionally add notes for the candidate, and confirm. The invite is added to your calendar (with a video link if Zoom is connected) and the candidate gets a confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Let Rose book automatically

Set your scheduling mode to **Automatic** in [Interview Preferences](/integrations/interview-preferences) and Rose books the first slot where you're both free — no action needed. Prefer to stay in control? Use **Semi-automatic** to have Rose suggest times for you to confirm.

<Callout kind="warning">
  Rose needs at least one connected calendar to book interviews. Set this up on the [Integrations](/integrations/scheduling) page.
</Callout>

Learn more in [Booking Interviews](/booking-interviews).
