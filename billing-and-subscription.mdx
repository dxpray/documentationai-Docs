Manage everything money-related from **Settings → Billing**, titled **Plans & credits**. From here you can see your current plan, track your credit usage, top up, and compare plans.

- **[Credits](/billing-and-subscription/credits)** — how usage is measured and topped up.
- **[Subscription Plans](/billing-and-subscription/plans)** — choosing the plan that fits your hiring volume.
