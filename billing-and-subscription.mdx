Manage your plan, credits, and billing details from the Settings page.

## Accessing Billing

Click **Settings** in the left sidebar, then select **Billing** from the settings menu.

## Credits

Hiros uses a credit system to track usage. Your current credit balance is always visible at the bottom of the left sidebar, along with the reset date.

For example: **3,950 / 4,000 left · Resets Jun 9**

Credits are consumed when candidates are contacted as part of a sequence. The exact credit cost per candidate depends on your plan.

If you are running low, click **Top up →** next to the credit counter to purchase additional credits.

<Callout kind="warning">
  Cancelling a sequence after it has launched does not refund credits already used on contacted candidates.
</Callout>

## Subscription Plans

Your subscription plan determines:

- Your monthly credit allowance
- The number of active sequences you can run simultaneously
- Access to features such as Reply Automation modes and ATS integrations
- Whether fully custom email sequences are available (Enterprise plans)

To view or change your plan, navigate to **Settings → Billing**.

For questions about enterprise pricing or custom plans, contact the Hiros team directly.
