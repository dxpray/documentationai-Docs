---
title: Roles & Permissions
description: Control what each member of your organisation can do.
---

Every member of your organisation is assigned a **role** that determines what they can access. Roles are shown next to each member in your organisation's member list, and you can change a member's role from there.

**Admins** have full access — to all features, settings, billing, and team management.

<Callout kind="info">
  Manage members and roles from the **organisation switcher** at the bottom of the left sidebar. From the member list you can also remove someone from the organisation.
</Callout>
