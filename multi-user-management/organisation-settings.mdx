---
title: Organisation Settings
description: Manage your organisation's profile and your own membership.
---

From your organisation's management screen — reached via the **organisation switcher** at the bottom of the left sidebar — you can manage the organisation itself.

- **Update your organisation profile** — such as its name and logo.
- **Leave the organisation** — remove your own access.
- **Delete the organisation** — permanently remove it and all associated data.

<Callout kind="warning">
  Deleting an organisation is irreversible and removes all associated data. Make sure this is what you intend before confirming.
</Callout>
