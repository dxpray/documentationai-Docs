---
title: Inviting Your Team
description: Bring colleagues into your organisation so they can collaborate on hiring.
---

To add colleagues, open the **organisation switcher** at the bottom of the left sidebar and choose to manage your organisation, then open its members.

## Sending invitations

Invite a colleague by entering their email address. They'll receive an invitation to join your organisation, and once they accept they'll share your sequences, candidates, and integrations.

Pending invitations are listed separately, where you can track or cancel them before they're accepted.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Working across multiple organisations? Use the same switcher to move between them — handy for recruiters and agencies serving several clients.
</Callout>
