When a candidate is ready to meet, Rose can match availability and book the call herself, or surface a ready-to-book candidate for you. You can always book manually too.

## In this section

- **[How Rose Books Interviews](/booking-interviews/how-rose-books)** — automated scheduling and the rules Rose follows.
- **[Booking Manually](/booking-interviews/booking-manually)** — picking a time yourself with the Book Intro Call flow.
- **[The Bookings Page](/booking-interviews/the-bookings-page)** — where all your scheduled interviews live.

<Callout kind="warning">
  Rose needs at least one connected calendar to book interviews. Set this up under [Scheduling Integrations](/integrations/scheduling).
</Callout>
