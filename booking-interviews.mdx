Once a candidate is ready to meet, booking is effortless. Rose can match availability and book the call herself, or surface a ready-to-book candidate for you — and you can always book manually from the Inbox or the Bookings page.

## How Rose books interviews

When a candidate shares availability, Rose doesn't just flag it — she works out exactly when the call should happen. She compares the candidate's stated times against your connected calendar, finds the windows where you're both genuinely free, and applies your scheduling rules before proposing or confirming anything.

Rose respects the guardrails you set in your interview preferences:

- **Buffers** between back-to-back calls
- **Minimum notice** before a call can be booked
- **A daily cap** on how many interviews can be scheduled
- **Time zones** — Rose books in the candidate's local time and confirms in yours

Depending on your interview preferences, Rose can handle scheduling three ways:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Automatic" icon="zap">
    Rose books the first slot where you're both free and confirms it immediately — calendar invite, video link, and confirmations to both sides, no action needed from you.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Semi-automatic" icon="list-checks">
    Rose offers you a shortlist of the best mutual slots (by email and, if connected, Slack) and waits for you to choose before confirming.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Manual" icon="hand">
    Rose notifies you that a candidate is ready and hands off, so you can pick the time and book it yourself.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

When a candidate is ready, Rose will:

1. Notify you that a candidate is ready to book (via email and, if connected, Slack)
2. Either book the call automatically, offer you a shortlist of times, or surface the conversation in your Inbox under **Needs you** — based on your settings

## Booking from the Inbox or Sequence Pipeline

To book manually, open the candidate's conversation. You'll see their full profile on the left and the conversation thread on the right. At the bottom, click **Book Intro Call**.

A calendar picker will open:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a date" icon="calendar">
    Available slots are pulled from your connected calendar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a time" icon="clock">
    30-minute slots are shown based on your availability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the booking summary" icon="check-circle">
    Confirms the candidate name, sequence, date, time, duration, timezone, and which calendar the event will be added to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add notes for the candidate" icon="edit">
    Optionally add preparation tips or topics you'd like to discuss ahead of the call.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm" icon="send">
    Click **Confirm** to finalise. The invite is automatically added to your calendar, and the candidate receives a confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The Bookings Page

Click **Bookings** in the left sidebar to see all your scheduled interviews in one place.

The page shows:

- Summary tiles: Today, Tomorrow, This Week, Confirmed, Past
- A filterable list of all interviews (Upcoming / Past / All)
- **Calendar Health** — a live status check of your connected calendar integrations (Google Calendar, Zoom, Outlook)

<Callout kind="warning">
  If your Calendar Health shows a disconnected integration, go to **Integrations** to reconnect it. Rose cannot book interviews without an active calendar connection.
</Callout>

## Adding a Candidate to Your ATS

From the candidate conversation panel, you can also click **Add to ATS** to push the candidate directly to your connected applicant tracking system. See [Integrations](/integrations) for setup.
