Once a candidate is ready to meet, booking is straightforward — either from the Inbox or from the Bookings page.

## How Booking Works

When a candidate expresses interest and shares their availability, Rose will:

1. Notify you that a candidate is ready to book (via email and, if connected, Slack)
2. Surface the conversation in your Inbox under **Needs you** (if you are on "Rose drafts, you approve" or "You reply yourself" mode) or handle it automatically (if on "Rose replies automatically")

## Booking from the Inbox or Sequence Pipeline

Open the candidate's conversation. You'll see their full profile on the left and the conversation thread on the right. At the bottom, click **Book Intro Call**.

A calendar picker will open:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a date" icon="calendar">
    Available slots are pulled from your connected calendar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a time" icon="clock">
    30-minute slots are shown based on your availability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the booking summary" icon="check-circle">
    Confirms the candidate name, sequence, date, time, duration, timezone, and which calendar the event will be added to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add notes for the candidate" icon="edit">
    Optionally add preparation tips or topics you'd like to discuss ahead of the call.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm" icon="send">
    Click **Confirm** to finalise. The invite is automatically added to your calendar, and the candidate receives a confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The Bookings Page

Click **Bookings** in the left sidebar to see all your scheduled interviews in one place.

The page shows:

- Summary tiles: Today, Tomorrow, This Week, Confirmed, Past
- A filterable list of all interviews (Upcoming / Past / All)
- **Calendar Health** — a live status check of your connected calendar integrations (Google Calendar, Zoom, Outlook)

<Callout kind="warning">
  If your Calendar Health shows a disconnected integration, go to **Integrations** to reconnect it. Rose cannot book interviews without an active calendar connection.
</Callout>

## Adding a Candidate to Your ATS

From the candidate conversation panel, you can also click **Add to ATS** to push the candidate directly to your connected applicant tracking system. See [Integrations](/integrations) for setup.
