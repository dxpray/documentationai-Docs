A **Sequence** holds everything Rose needs to source and reach out for one role — the job details, the outreach emails, and the pipeline of candidates for that hire. Click **Sequences** in the left sidebar to see them all.

## In this section

- **[Creating a Sequence](/sequences/creating-a-sequence)** — the three-step setup, including uploading a job description.
- **[Managing a Sequence](/sequences/managing-a-sequence)** — the tabs inside a sequence and how the candidate pipeline flows.
- **[The Outreach Emails](/sequences/outreach-emails)** — the three-email sequence Rose sends, and how to edit it.
- **[Sequence Statuses](/sequences/sequence-statuses)** — what Draft, In Progress, Completed, and the rest mean.

## The Sequences page

The page opens with **Your Sequences** and three headline stats across the top:

| Stat | What it shows |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Active** | How many sequences are currently in progress |
| **Reply rate** | The share of contacted candidates who have replied |
| **Interviews booked** | Total interviews booked across your sequences |

Below that, each sequence appears as a row showing its name, creator, location, reply count, interview count, and current status. Use the **Filter** control to view by status, and **New Sequence** (top right) to start a new one.

<Callout kind="tip">
  New to Hiros? The Sequences page walks you through the flow: **JD → Brief → Sourcing → Outreach**. Set up a sequence first, and your candidate shortlists get sharper.
</Callout>
