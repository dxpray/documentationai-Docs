Sequences are how Rose reaches out to your selected candidates. Each sequence is tied to a specific role and contains all the outreach emails, job details, and pipeline tracking for that hire.

## The Sequences Page

Click **Sequences** in the left sidebar to see all your sequences. The overview shows:

- **Active** — number of sequences currently in progress
- **Reply Rate** — percentage of contacted candidates who have replied across all sequences
- **Interviews Booked** — total interviews booked

Each row in the table shows the sequence name, creator, location, reply count, interview count, and current status.

## Creating a New Sequence

Click **+ New Sequence** in the top right. You'll be guided through three steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="The Position" icon="briefcase">
    Enter the Campaign Title (internal only — not shared with candidates), Job Title, Company Name, and a description of the role and company.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Work Arrangement" icon="map-pin">
    Select the employment type (full-time, part-time, contract), work policy (remote, hybrid, on-site), and location.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Role Details" icon="file-text">
    Add compensation details, key responsibilities, and what you're looking for in a candidate. You can also upload a job description to auto-populate these fields.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  The more detail you provide, the better Rose's outreach emails will be. Candidates Rose engages may not be actively job hunting — a compelling, specific description of the opportunity is what converts them.
</Callout>

## Sequence Statuses

| Status | Description |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Draft** | Created but not yet launched. You can still edit all details. |
| **In Progress** | The sequence is live. Candidates are receiving emails. |
| **Completed** | All candidates have received all emails in the sequence. Rose will still notify you if a completed-sequence candidate replies. |

Cancelling a sequence does not restore used candidate or email credits, depending on your plan.

## Adding Candidates to a Sequence

You can add candidates to a sequence at any point — before or after creating it. From the Rose AI search or Manual Filters results, select candidates and choose which sequence to add them to.

## Managing a Sequence

From within a sequence, you can access five tabs:

- **Pipeline** — a full list of all candidates in the sequence, showing their current role, stage (Replied, Interested, Not interested, etc.), and last activity timestamp.
- **Interviews booked** — all interviews that have been scheduled with candidates from this sequence.
- **Outreach** — the three-email sequence Rose will send (or has sent) to candidates. You can view and edit the email copy here before launching.
- **Job Details** — the role information you entered during setup, plus the **Reply Automation** setting.
- **ATS Integration** — push candidates directly to your connected ATS from within the sequence.

## The Outreach Emails

Rose generates a personalised three-touch email sequence based on your job details:

- **Email 1 — Initial Outreach** — introduces Rose, explains why she's reaching out, and pitches the opportunity
- **Email 2 — Follow-up** — a shorter follow-up sent a few days later
- **Email 3 — Final Follow-up** — a final, brief touchpoint

Emails are sent over 6–7 days, excluding Sundays.

Tokens like `{{firstName}}` are automatically personalised per candidate by Rose. The opening and closing sections of each email are managed by Rose and are not editable, as they are optimised for deliverability. The main body of each email can be edited to reflect your preferences.

<Callout kind="warning">
  When editing email copy, avoid hype language ("revolutionary", "game-changing", "once-in-a-lifetime") and urgency or pressure tactics ("last chance", "urgent response needed"). These patterns reduce email deliverability and candidate response rates.
</Callout>

## Reply Automation

Found in the **Job Details** tab of each sequence, Reply Automation controls how Rose handles candidate replies:

- **Rose replies automatically** — Rose manages the full conversation end-to-end, escalating only genuinely complex situations to you
- **Rose drafts, you approve** — Rose drafts every reply, but nothing is sent until you approve it in the Inbox
- **You reply yourself** — Rose stays silent; you take over all replies manually

You can change this setting at any time, even after a sequence has launched.
